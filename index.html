PROJECT IDEA

(CHRISTINA NUNEZ, 2019) As the world seeks to slow the pace of climate change, preserve wildlife, and support billions of people, trees inevitably hold a significant part of the answer.
However, preserving forests is not easy, especially forest fire. In the 21st century, a forest fire is a severe problem. The global governances do not have precise solutions to prevent a forest from a fire-affected by natural and human activities factors.

(Julia Perez, 2017) commented that an uncontrolled forest fire can devastate everything in its path, spread for miles, crossing rivers and roads. Each year, between 60,000 and 80,000 forest fires occur, destroying between 3 and 10 million hectares. While forest fires have different environmental impacts, depending on their size and frequency, the solution is still a big question unsolved.

That is why I came up with an application to prevent the forest from a serious fire.
Project Name: FAP

a. Overview:

FAP is an application which stands for Fire Alarm App, is an essential application for preserving and preventing the forests from fire. The project including Arduino, module sim and GPS, and a sensor device. The sensor device would be responsible for collecting the data around the device and send back to the Arduino system before the system accomplish the final step of sending the alarm to the relevant audience. The most crucial target is to warn the relevant audience of some unusual situation in the forest to give some practical solutions and reduce disasters as quickly as possible. On the other hand, the fire alarm app also focuses on mining data for research and predicting weather phenomena, supporting clarifying some problems related to nature and environmental factors.

b. Motivation:


Wildfire is a global situation in which any national government does not have specific solutions to this issue. The Fire alarm app is simple, but its vital functions and meaningful purpose are why it stays here. According to the chart research from National Interagency Fire Center, during the period 1983- 2021, show the total number of wildfires per year is as presented below:

During this period, just in the United States, National Interagency Fire Center confirmed an average of 70,000 forest fires per year. However, The Forest Service has shown that the number of wildfires would be even higher than the data processed from the National Interagency. Indeed, there is no specific number to show the clear impacts related to humans and life there. Everything the human should do is to the preservation and ready at any emergency time. According to the market, the price of the sensor is reasonable, adapt to the budget of the government’s finance in preserving the forest. The Fire alarm app is an example of solving emergencies via timely solutions.

c. Descriptions:


The fire alarm app is a project including sensor device including position sensor, temperature sensor, and solar battery which is set up into a device; 2 kinds of the module including Module Sim and Module GPS; the Arduinos, an application to process the data from the Arduino and send to the relevant audience.

The solar battery is used in sensor devices to supply boundless energy. The power is given from the sunlight, especially in dangerous places such as forests that humans may have some difficulties approaching and fixing if necessary.

Before the wildfire appears, the temperature may increase temporarily, by the nature factor until the forest fire happens, the sensor device will send the clue to the Arduino. During the period from abnormal temperature change to the appearance of the fire, this is an example of big data to show more evidence and time for the relevant audience to solve the problems.

At this time, the Arduino sends the data to module GPS and module Sim. While module GPS is responsible for identifying the position of forest fire, module Sim issues a warning via a message or emergency notification.

The Fire alarm app is set up to code the back-end. When the data and clue are given from the Arduino via module Sim and module GPS, the application can process the data. However, the primary function is to warn the position on Map and abnormal temperature, leading to fire hazards. Fire alarm app also provides a solution via AI technology, meaning the application would save previous data, compared new data, and give the optimal solution based on different situations. For example, the application would scan multiple times to check the amplitude change of the temperature. If the temperature range abnormal threshold exceeds (over 40 degrees), the application instantly issues a warning to related objects.
The Application has completely accomplished the most important mission of issuing a warning to relevant audiences as soon as possible to give them the valuable time to give the best solutions for the emergency. After issuing a warning, all functions return to the initial step forever.

On the other hand, the application is also helpful for the forest every day. The sensor device would scan multiple time, send the data to the Arduino, process the data and send to the phone related via module Sim and module GPS. This storing data method is an example of big data where temperature, GPS, and time are updated each second. It is considered a tool to support seismologists and geographers to research and discover areas with unique structures and properties. Scientists would have robust data to predict the different situations based on seasons, months, and periods in the long term. As a result, the human can reduce the riskiness of the forest fire.

In conclusion, The Fire Alarm Application is an essential tool to issue a warning fire from the forest to relevant audiences and support them give timely solutions to reduce the dire situation as soon as possible. On the other hand, the application can collect the data to provide the chart and different kinds of information via periods such as a month, year for scientists’ researching demand and create predictions to prevent severe disasters in the future.
